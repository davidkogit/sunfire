The Amplifier

Engineered for One Purpose

The HRSIW8AMP is not a general-purpose amplifier. It was designed from the ground up to power only the Sunfire HRSIW8 in-wall subwoofer — delivering 520 watts RMS of clean, distortion-free bass through a compact 1U rack-mount chassis.

Amplifier Detail Shot 800 × 600px

Class-D Discrete Design

All-discrete Class-D switching amplifier for maximum robustness and efficiency. 75W average consumption, less than 0.5W in standby.

DSP Equalization

Full digital signal processing with adjustable crossover (40–160Hz), selectable slopes (12–36 dB/octave), subsonic filter, and phase control.

StillBass Technology

Sunfire's exclusive anti-shake technology eliminates mechanical vibration, delivering powerful bass without disturbing your living space.

Smart Standby

Intelligent power management servo-controls the main amplifier, drawing less than half a watt from household mains during standby mode.

The Science

Three Technologies.
One Extraordinary Result.

High Back-EMF Technology 600 × 400px
01

High Back-EMF

Sunfire's High Back-EMF Technology produces massive amounts of deep bass from a cabinet not much larger than the woofer itself. By increasing input power by a factor of 10 or more and designing custom drivers with oversized magnets and voice coils, Sunfire achieves what conventional designs cannot: efficiency, small enclosure, and bass extension — all three.

This is the science behind Hoffman's Iron Law — and how Sunfire breaks it.

Learn more about High Back-EMF
StillBass Anti-Shake Technology 600 × 400px
02

StillBass®

What allows the HRSIW8 to deliver room-shaking bass without shaking your walls? StillBass — Sunfire's exclusive mechanical vibration elimination technology. Originally developed for the legendary SubRosa, StillBass packs more power and performance into in-wall subwoofers than any competitive product.

Powerful bass. Zero vibration. Your neighbors will never know.

Frequency Filtration Design 600 × 400px
03

Frequency Filtration Design

Proprietary FFD Technology is what makes the HRSIW8AMP the perfect match for the prodigious bass capabilities of the HRSIW8 subwoofer. Combined with DSP-controlled equalization, adjustable crossover, and five EQ presets (Impact, Movie, Music, Dynamic, Flat), every listening experience is precisely tuned.

Five EQ presets. Three memory locations. Infinite possibilities.
